Always in the market
The most talented lawyers, auditors, and tax advisors are rarely available — and almost never visible. They are not on job boards, they are not responding to LinkedIn messages, and they are not engaging with recruiters they do not know or trust. Reaching them takes relationships built over time, consistent presence in the market, and the kind of sector focus that makes candidates feel understood rather than targeted.
We always know who is open to a move
Most recruiters start searching when you brief them. We don't. Our goal is to know, at any given moment, which candidates across law, audit, and tax advisory are open to a conversation, whether they are actively looking, passively considering their options, or simply at a stage in their career where the right opportunity would be hard to ignore. We invest heavily in maintaining those relationships so that when you come to us with a requirement, we are not starting from scratch. We already have the market mapped. This means faster results, better candidates, and a process that does not rely on hoping the right person happens to see a job advert at the right time.
A process that keeps the ball moving
Speed matters in recruitment — the best candidates do not stay available for long. That is why every search we run follows a structured, proven process where every member of our team knows exactly what needs to happen and when. You will always know where things stand, what has been done, and what comes next. Our relentless work ethic means we are always pushing the process forward — no delays, no dropped balls, no excuses.

Let’s talk about your next move How we work

Market knowledge

When you speak to TJC Partners, you are not speaking to a generalist recruiter who has just been briefed on a role. You are speaking to a team that lives and breathes law, audit, and tax advisory. We understand the firms, the cultures, the career paths, and the opportunities that exist right now — and the ones that are coming.

Real relationships

We are not interested in placing you in a role and moving on. Our business is built on long-term relationships with candidates at every stage of their career, from newly qualified professionals taking their first step to senior partners considering their next chapter. We take the time to understand not just what you do, but what you want.

Help you stand out

The best opportunities attract strong competition. We work closely with every candidate we represent to make sure you are positioned in the best possible light — from how your experience is presented to how you are introduced to a potential employer. We know what our clients are looking for and we know how to articulate your value in a way that resonates.

Total discretion

We understand that exploring a career move is a sensitive matter. Whether you are quietly considering your options or actively looking for a change, everything you share with us stays strictly confidential. We will never approach a firm on your behalf, share your details, or disclose that you are in the market without your full knowledge and consent. Your career, your timeline, your terms.
